What We Do

Below are the services we offer our clients,
each custom tailored to suit your specific needs.

Web Design & Development

Our web design and development solutions are completely customized for your business. So are the internet marketing strategies we offer. Whether it is your first online presence, in need of a website refresh, seeking to better manage your web content, trying to increase visibility online, or looking for a way to move from traditional print media to digital, MMG Creative has the experience to make it happen.

Video Editing & DVD Authoring

MMG Creative makes it affordable to get professional Video Editing, Authoring, Menu Design, and Post-Production services for your DVD or Blu-ray. We also customize your release by adding options such as Dolby 5.1 Surround, Director Commentary, Behind the Scenes footage, Motion Menus, and more. Whether you are planning your release or putting the final touches on your project, we provide the assistance and expertise you need to create a professional and functional DVD or Blu-ray.

Branding, Marketing & Design

We know your brand is more than a logo. It is the heart of your company, how others think of you and how you distinguish your identity. It is the foundation for growth. All of this is how we help you to build your business. MMG Creative develops strategic was for growth and prepare a plan that matches your vision and produces the advantages and competitive edge you need.

Film Budgeting & Scheduling

Making a small independent short or feature film is no different that a big budget Hollywood movie. The same steps are needed to bring in your project on time and on budget. MMG Creative has the solutions you need to get this done. From Breakdown sheets, Stripboards, Storyboards, Shoot days and locations, we have you completely covered. Doing your homework before setting foot on set makes your production process that much easier!
